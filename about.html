About this site

Caring for freshwater shrimp species for the first time might seem like a daunting feat at first.
This is why I made this website. I myself am caring for a tank of my own, and this site will be filled with information from my experiences.
Although this website might not have information that's completely helpful or accurate, I hope it helps you in some way.

Even though it might be challenging to start, eventually- if you get things right- your tank will be a booming paradise of active, healthy, breeding shrimp.
